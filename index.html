My story

I grew up surrounded by videogames, movies and cartoons. Eventually I would find myself drawing non-stop, inspired by everything surrounding me. Growing up I started to take an interest in others forms of art like filmmaking, music, photography, 3D. But everything always led me to animation, it's a combination of all the things I love and it can be applied to all of the fields I love. After a few years of trying and failing miserably, eventually I made it and now here I am.
